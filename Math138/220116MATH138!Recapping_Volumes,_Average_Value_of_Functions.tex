	\h{Average Volume of a Function}
	
	The \textbf{Average value} of $f(x)$ from $x=a$ to $x=b$ is:\\\\
	$\displaystyle f_{\text{avg}} = \frac{1}{b-a} \int_a^b f(x)\; dx$
